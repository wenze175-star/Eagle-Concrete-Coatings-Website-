About Eagle Concrete Coatings

Built on Craftsmanship,
Not Shortcuts

Eagle Concrete Coatings was founded by Colton Kunkel with one goal: deliver the best concrete floor coating in the South Metro and stand behind every single job. Colton started the business because he saw too many homeowners stuck with subpar coatings installed by fly-by-night crews using inferior materials.

We use a premium polyurea chip system that is stronger, more flexible, and more UV-stable than traditional epoxy. It cures fast enough for us to complete most jobs in a single day, and it's built to last 20+ years in Minnesota's demanding climate.
