Garage Floor Coatings

Your garage floor takes more abuse than almost any other surface in your home — hot tires, road salt, oil spills, and constant foot traffic. Our polyurea chip system is engineered to handle all of it without peeling, cracking, or yellowing.

Unlike big-box epoxy kits that fail in 1–3 years, our professional polyurea system bonds directly to the concrete at a molecular level and flexes with the slab as temperatures change — a critical feature in Minnesota.

  • Installed and ready to use within 24 hours
  • 4x stronger than standard epoxy systems
  • UV-stable — won't yellow or fade in sunlight
  • Slip-resistant broadcast flake texture
  • Resistant to hot tires, motor oil, road salt, and chemicals
  • Available in 12 decorative color blends
  • Crack and joint repair included in prep process

Patio & Outdoor Surfaces

Outdoor concrete takes a beating — UV rays bleach it, freeze-thaw cycles crack it, and moisture stains it. Our polyurea coating system is specifically formulated for outdoor use and outperforms every alternative available to Minnesota homeowners.

Whether it's a patio, front stoop, pool deck, or driveway apron, we prep the surface properly and apply a coating system that bonds permanently and stays beautiful through decades of Minnesota seasons.

  • 100% UV-stable — no fading, bleaching, or yellowing
  • Engineered to handle freeze-thaw cycles
  • Slip-resistant texture — safe when wet
  • Resists moss, mildew, and moisture penetration
  • Transforms cracked, stained slabs into clean surfaces
  • 12 color options including natural stone-look blends

Basement Floors

Most basement floors are bare, grey, and damp — not exactly inviting. A polyurea floor coating transforms your basement into a space you actually want to use: clean, bright, moisture-resistant, and easy to maintain.

Our system creates a seamless, non-porous surface that resists moisture vapor, inhibits mold growth, and reflects light — making even dark basements feel larger and cleaner.

  • Moisture-resistant barrier — protects against vapor and seepage
  • Inhibits mold and mildew growth
  • Bright, reflective finish opens up dark spaces
  • Easy to clean — no grout lines, no porous surface
  • Durable enough for home gym, workshop, or living space
  • Installed in one day — minimal disruption

Commercial & Industrial Coatings

Commercial floors face demands that residential systems can't handle — forklifts, chemical spills, constant foot traffic, and the pressure of zero downtime. Our industrial polyurea systems are rated for heavy commercial use and installed fast enough to get your business back to full operation quickly.

We work with auto shops, retail spaces, warehouses, showrooms, restaurants, and light industrial facilities throughout the South Metro.

  • Heavy-traffic and forklift rated
  • Superior chemical and solvent resistance
  • OSHA-compliant slip resistance
  • Fast cure — minimize business downtime
  • Seamless, easy-to-clean surface for food service and medical
  • Withstands heavy equipment and vehicle traffic
  • Custom color zones available for safety marking

How It Works

From First Call to Finished Floor:
Our 4-Step Process

We make it easy. Most jobs are completed start to finish in a single day.

01

Free Quote

Colton visits your property, assesses the concrete condition, and provides a detailed quote — usually within 48 hours of your call.

02

Surface Preparation

We diamond grind the concrete to open the pores, repair cracks and spalls, and ensure the surface is perfectly clean and ready for coating.

03

Coating Day

We apply the polyurea base coat, broadcast the decorative flake blend of your choice, and seal everything with a durable topcoat — all in one visit.

04

Ready in 24 Hours

Your floor is ready for foot traffic same evening and vehicle traffic within 24 hours. No week-long wait, no strong fumes lingering for days.

FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to what homeowners ask most before booking their coating.

Pricing varies based on the size of the space, condition of the concrete, and any prep work needed. A standard 2-car garage typically ranges from $1,500–$2,500. Colton provides a free, itemized quote so you know exactly what you're getting before committing.
You can walk on the floor the same evening of installation. You can park your vehicle on it within 24 hours. This is one of the biggest advantages of polyurea over epoxy — epoxy can require 5–7 days of cure time before vehicle traffic.
For garage floors, weather typically doesn't affect installation since it's a controlled interior environment. For outdoor surfaces, we do monitor humidity and temperature and will reschedule if conditions aren't ideal. We'll always give you advance notice.
Yes — crack and joint repair is included in our preparation process. We fill and repair cracks and spalls before coating so the finished surface looks smooth and clean. Very severe structural cracks may require additional discussion, but most standard cracking is handled in prep.
Our polyurea system is designed to last 20+ years with normal residential use. Commercial applications vary based on traffic intensity, but our systems routinely outlast epoxy by 3–5x. Unlike DIY kits, professional polyurea doesn't delaminate or peel under normal conditions.
Yes — the entire garage floor needs to be clear of vehicles, shelving, and items on the floor on installation day. We'll discuss logistics during your quote. Most customers move items to their driveway or yard for the day. We handle the rest.
Once cured, our polyurea coatings are completely safe for pets and children. There is a brief period during installation where the space should be well-ventilated, but polyurea cures much faster than epoxy and off-gasses significantly less. Your family can use the space normally within 24 hours.
